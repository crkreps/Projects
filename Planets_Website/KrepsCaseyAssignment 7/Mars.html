Mars

×

This is Mars, also known as the Red Planet. Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun. It is 142 million miles from the Sun. The length of a day on Mars is 24 hours and 37 minutes. A year is 687 Earth days. An average temperature is around -81 degrees F. "(Facts from Nasa.gov)" Currently a group of engineers and scientists are working on a mission to establish human esttlement on mars. This project is known as Mars One. In 2026 the first crew will launch into space on a seven month long journey. The crew will experience gravity that is 62.5% less than Earth's. This is truly a revolutionary time in human history.
